Baked Cheesecake

Description

This recipe for baked cheesecake has been taken from RecipeTin Eats. At the time of writing, the recipe has 5.0 stars from 110 reviews. It has a biscuit base, a smooth and rich filling and berry toppings that come together to create a delicious but not overly sweet dessert.

Ingredients

Biscuit Base

  • 200 g milk arrowroot biscuits
  • 8 tbsp unsalted butter (melted)

Filling

  • 500 g cream cheese (softened)
  • 2 tbsp plain flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 125 g sour cream
  • 300 g caster sugar
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 3 large eggs (room temperature)

Toppings

  • Strawberries
  • Blueberries
  • Raspberries
  • Icing sugar (for dusting)

Steps

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 160°C (140°C fan).
  2. Get a 20 cm springform cake tin. Turn the base upside down, butter lightly and place a square piece of parchment/baking paper on the base and clip into the springform pan. Excess paper will stick out. Butter and line the side of the pan.

Biscuit Base

  1. Break up biscuits roughly by hand and place in a food processor. Blitz until fine crumbs. Add butter and briefly blitz until dispersed and it resembles wet sand.
  2. Pour into the prepared cake tin. Use a spatula to roughly spread it out over the base and up the walls. Use something with a flat base and vertical edges (I used a measuring cup) to press the crumbs up the wall almost to the top of the sides, and flatten the base.

Filling

  1. Use a mixer or beater to beat the cream cheese until smooth (about 30 seconds on medium high).
  2. Add flour and beat until just combined (10 seconds). Add vanilla, sour cream, sugar and lemon zest. Beat until just combined (10-15 seconds). Add eggs one at a time, beating between until just combined (10 seconds each). Don't over beat, we don't want to aerate the batter.
  3. Pour into prepared crust and bake for 55 minutes. The top should be a very light golden brown, not cracked, and near perfectly flat. It should jiggle slightly when you gently shake the pan.
  4. Cool in the oven with the door open then refrigerate for at least 4 hours in the pan.
  5. Remove the springform pan sides and use the overhanging paper to slide the cheesecake off the cake pan, then slide the cheesecake off the paper.
  6. Top with berries and dust with icing sugar.
